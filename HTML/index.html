Simplicity is key.

We believe in building from the ground up. Firstly we will analyze the current state of your architecture, determine the long term viability of the solution and stabilize the foundations of your company. Once an architecture is modelled adequately, the developers can organically access the necessary building blocks they need to write stable applications that scale your business.

Architecture

We will consider modern approaches depending on the business need. Explicitly has proven experience in powerful approaches such as Event Sourcing and microservices.

Development

Features can be developed for your business in a way that can be supported and maintained over time. We will use strategies such as TDD, Functional Programming, API Documentation, UML which will help your business extend the applications with new features over time

Agile delivery

We are scrum certified and will endeavour to deliver the smallest amounts of value all the way to production during an agreed sprint period. We will endeavour to provide the business with granular delivery metrics such as velocity, throughput

Pair Programming

We embrace pairing when it makes sense. In the past, we have found that onboarding processes can be rapidly streamlined as well as at the beginning phases of new projects.

Leadership

We can provide a Team Lead / CTO function to your business. Explicitly has years of experience in guiding teams and interviewing candidates. We will find new team members of a suitable mindset that will aid the business long term

Mentoring

Attracting young talent and keeping them at a business can be quite a challenge. We embrace fresh ideas and inquisitive minds. Using code reviews, pair programming, task delegation we can ensure Junior team members remain motivated and feel challenged on a daily basis
